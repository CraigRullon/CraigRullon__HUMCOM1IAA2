MY HOBBY DURING PANDEMIC

Video Games

Ever since before I love playing video games but the game called Valorant remove my boredness during pandemic. So what is valorant?. Valorant is a free-to-play first-person hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Microsoft Windows and probably MAC OS. The always have 5 man team and also vs 5 man team. People can communicate through Voice Chat. This feature create more experience for me there are many people that I meet and became friends with.

Watching Netflix

Everybody knows netflix. Netflix is subricption-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies without commercials on an internet-connected device. Members can also download TV shows and movies on IOS, Android, or Windows device and can be watch without an internet connection. Community, Bojack HorseMan, and castlevania are my favorite TV shows. Netflix really release help me on my boredness
