Step by Step Tutorial

Cooking Rice

  1. Measure the cup of rice you want to eat.
  2. Wash the rice with clean water and drain the container.
  3. This time put water again but measure it (ex. 4cup of rice = 2cup of water).
  4. Put it in the rice cooker and leave until its done.

Cooking Scramble Egg

  1. Get an egg in the fridge!.
  2. Crack the egg in half.
  3. Put the egg in a bowl.
  4. Start steering it in a circular motion with a fork.
  5. Put a magic sarap or a little bit of salt.
  6. Get a cooking pan.
  7. Open your stove and put it in a minimal level.
  8. Put a small amount of oil the pan
  9. Wait for 30 second
  10. Put the egg inside the pan.
  11. Cook it for a minute or two.
